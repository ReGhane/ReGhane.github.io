    About me

    I am a third year Ph.D student of Electrical Engineering at Caltech where I am grateful to be advised by Babak Hassibi. I am interested in the theory of deep learning, random matrix theory, control theory, and signal processing.

    I received my M.Sc degree in Electrical Engineering from California Institute of Technology in 2024 and my B.Sc in Electrical Engineering and Mathematics from Sharif University of Technology in 2022.
